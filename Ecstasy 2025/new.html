ABOUT ECSTASY

Ecstasy, the cultural festival of the Central Institute of Technology Kokrajhar, is one of the most prominent fests in lower Assam. Known for drawing thousands of students, artists, and attendees from across the country, the event promises an unforgettable experience. This year, it is set to take place on the 8th and 9th of February, with expectations of an even greater crowd than ever before.

Sports Events

Ecstasy 2025 offers thrilling sports competitions, including basketball, football, cricket, and more. Get ready to witness high-energy matches and cheer for your favorite teams!

Cultural Events

Ecstasy 2025 features vibrant dance, music, drama, and art performances, showcasing talent and creativity from across the country.

Competitions

Ecstasy 2025 hosts exciting competitions in dance, music, art, quizzes, and more. Showcase your talent and compete with the best from across the country!
